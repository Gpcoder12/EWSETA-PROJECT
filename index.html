Welcome to the EWSETA Career Guidance Portal

About US

As one of South Africa's 21 SETAs the Energy and Water Sector Education and Training Authority(EWSETA)was established in terms of Section 9 of skills development Act(SDA)97 of 1998 as amended and reports and reports to the Minister of Higher Education Science and Technology

This portal aims to educate website visitors about the various opportunities in the Enery and Water sector in South Africa
